How to Play:

  1. To start playing, first place a bet by selecting the desired amount of gold and then choose the number of mines you want to have on the arena. Once you have made your choices, press the "play" button to begin.
  2. Your goal is to look for bounties hidden within the arena while avoiding the mines that are also hidden within the same space. To search for bounties, simply click on any square on the arena.
  3. Be aware that the higher the number of bounties on the arena, the higher the odds of winning. However, this also means that there is a higher likelihood of there being mines nearby, so proceed with caution.
  4. If you accidentally step on a mine, you will lose all of the gold you have accumulated thus far. To prevent this from happening, it is best to cash out your winnings before stepping on a mine.
  5. Once you have cashed out or detonated a mine, the game will end, and you can start again by placing another bet and selecting the number of mines you want to play with.
  6. Win the game by raising 15000 Gold
